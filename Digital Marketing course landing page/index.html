What are the benefits of the DMI membership?

Exclusive content library designed around you

The content library is extensive and designed to help in your day-to-day job.

The platform is full of content, with information and up-to-date knowledge and techniques from DMI alumni, practitioners and subject matter experts.

Connected to a range of tools, designed to help you succeed

Access to expert content, to guide your career forwards, as well as high-quality templates use at work. You also have access to a Career Zone, for support and professional development, alongside learning activities via webinars and online programs.

Continuous Professional Development (CPD)

As a digital marketing professional, you’ll know that skills and knowledge need to be updated frequently, to keep up with the ever-changing world of digital marketing. The DMI membership enables you to align yourself with the latest skills, knowledge and trends, while expanding the lifespan of your certification through an exclusive MyCPD area.
